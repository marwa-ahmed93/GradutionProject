The popularity of online applications, and particularly web-based information systems, is influenced by a variety of factors. The ability to update and maintain without distributing and installing software on tens of thousands of client PCs is the major justification. Therefore, we developed a website that makes it simpler and faster for staff and students to access college services. There is less need for direct communication with employees, particularly during the Corona pandemic. Students and faculty can assign orders to the appropriate departments using their accounts and watch the progress of those orders until they are ready to be filled. For the majority of the services it already offers, the website may also offer an online payment option.
